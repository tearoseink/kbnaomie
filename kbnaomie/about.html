I started A Backyard Art Show in 2022 with the help of my friends, Jazzy and Franz. The goal was a community event to support conversations, connections, and a love of painting.

Key features include the show starting outdoors at sunset, festival vibes late into the night, colorful lights, a 5 hour music playlist, and tigers.

- KBNAOMIE
